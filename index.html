Privacy Policy

Capora — Personal Finance App · Last updated: March 21, 2026

In short: Capora does not sell, share, or analyze your financial data. All data stays on your device. Cloud sync is optional and entirely under your control.

1. What Data We Collect

Capora stores the following data that you enter manually:

  • Transactions (amount, description, category, date)
  • Recurring expenses and income
  • Savings goals (jars)
  • App settings (starting balance, planned income, budgets)

Capora does not automatically collect any data. There is no analytics, no location tracking, no access to contacts, SMS, camera, or any other device sensors.

2. Where Data Is Stored

Locally on your device. All data is primarily stored in the app's local storage (localStorage) on your Android device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync.

3. Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable synchronization, your data is stored on Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud infrastructure) under a unique user ID.

  • Anonymous sync — data is synchronized without any personal information. No name, email, or other identifiers are stored.
  • Google Account sync — if you connect a Google account, your email address is stored solely for authentication purposes. Your email is never used for marketing or shared with third parties.

You can disconnect your Google account and delete all cloud data at any time through the app settings or by contacting the developer.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

Capora does not share data with any third parties. We do not use:

  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • User tracking tools
  • External APIs that access user data

5. Data Security

  • Local data is protected by Android operating system security mechanisms.
  • Cloud data is protected by Firebase Security Rules — each user can only access their own data.
  • All communication with Firebase services uses HTTPS encryption.
  • The app does not store passwords, card numbers, IBANs, or other sensitive financial identifiers.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • View all your data — it is visible within the app.
  • Export your data in JSON or CSV format using the Export option in the app.
  • Delete all your data using the Reset option in Settings.
  • Disconnect Cloud Sync and request deletion of cloud data.

To delete cloud data or for any questions, contact us at the email below.

7. Children

Capora is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
