Privacy Policy

Capora — Personal Finance App · Last updated: March 30, 2026

In short: Capora does not sell your data. By default, your finance data stays on your device. Cloud Sync is OFF by default and is only used if you explicitly enable it in Settings. If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is processed and stored by Google Firebase so synchronization can work.

1. What Data We Collect

Capora stores the data you manually enter, such as:

  • Transactions (amount, description, category, date)
  • Recurring expenses and income
  • Savings goals / jars
  • App settings (starting balance, budgets, notification preferences, etc.)

Capora does not use analytics or advertising trackers. We do not collect location data and we do not access contacts, SMS, camera, microphone, or similar device sensors.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Capora may request the Android Notifications permission. Notification scheduling is performed by the app on your device. We do not receive your notification content.

Household / Shared data (only if you use it)

If you use any “household”, “sharing”, or similar collaboration feature, the data you choose to sync/share can be visible to the other members you add or invite. Only invite people you trust.

2. Where Data Is Stored

Local (default)

Your data is primarily stored locally on your Android device (app local storage). This data does not leave your device unless you enable Cloud Sync.

Note about device backups: Depending on your Android/Google backup settings, app data may be included in system backups that are managed by your device/Google account.

Cloud Sync (optional)

Cloud Sync is disabled by default. If you explicitly enable synchronization in Settings, your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud):

  • Firebase Firestore (for synced app data)
  • Firebase Authentication (to identify your sync account)

When using Cloud Sync, Google/Firebase may process technical information necessary to provide the service (e.g., IP address and standard service logs).

3. Cloud Sync Options

Cloud Sync is only activated after you explicitly enable it in Settings. When Cloud Sync is off, Capora does not connect to Firebase for synchronization.

  • Anonymous sync: synchronization without a personal profile inside the app.
  • Google Account sync: if you connect Google Sign‑In, your Google account identifier (such as email) may be processed by Firebase Authentication for login purposes. It is not used for marketing.

You can disable Cloud Sync and delete cloud data via the app (when available) or by contacting the developer.

4. Third Parties

Capora does not sell your personal data and does not use advertising networks.

Service providers (only when needed for features you enable):

  • Google Firebase (Auth / Firestore) for optional Cloud Sync.

We do not use:

  • Analytics (including Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reporting (including Crashlytics)
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Data brokers

5. Data Security

  • Local data is protected by Android operating system mechanisms.
  • Cloud data (if enabled) is protected by Firebase Security Rules designed to restrict access to authorized users only.
  • Communication with Firebase services uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Capora does not store passwords, card numbers, IBANs, or similar sensitive banking identifiers.

If the app includes an app lock / PIN feature, the app stores only a verification value (e.g., a hash) locally—not the raw PIN.

6. Your Rights and Controls

You can:

  • View your data inside the app
  • Export your data (e.g., JSON/CSV, if the feature is available in your build)
  • Delete local app data (Reset)
  • Enable/disable Cloud Sync
  • Request deletion of cloud data

For deletion requests or privacy questions, contact the developer.

7. Children

Capora is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
